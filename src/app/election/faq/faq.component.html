1. What is Election Ke Bazigaar?

Election ke Bazigaar is a Free game where you predict which major party will win how many seats in Uttar Pradesh, Punjab, Uttarakhand and Goa Elections 2022 and win up to Rs 2.5 Lacs!!! More than 120 prizes on offer. Hurry!

2. Is the game free or paid?

The entire game is FREE to play.

3. How will I know if I have won the prize?

If you have won a prize, we will contact you over phone/SMS before 18th March, 2022.

4. When will the game results get declared?

Check your RANK and D.SCORE on March 11th, 2022 after the Election results are announced. Remember to save/bookmark this game link so you can check on March 11th if you have won a prize. Happy Playing!

5. Can I change my prediction?

You can change your predictions whenever you want, as many times as you want, until 6th March, 2022

6. Can I win more than one prize?

Only one prize per Mobile Number is eligible. You may play with different Mobile Numbers but you can win a maximum of one prize.

7. Do I need to be a resident of the state for which I am predicting? For example, if I am a resident of Karnataka, can I predict for Uttar Pradesh?

YES! You can predict for any or all states as long as you are an Indian resident of any state.

8. How do I maximise my chances of winning a prize?

Firstly, predict for all states instead of just 1 or 2. Secondly, read ABP news. Do some research on which parties are forming what kind of alliances and competing on how many seats. Check past election results.

9. How are the winners decided?

The closer you are able to tell the results based upon your knowledge about the subject matter and in depth understanding about the Constituencies/States and all such other related information/facts which shall have a material bearing on the outcome, the higher your rank and the more you are likely to win a prize.

10. Why do I need to Login/provide my Mobile Number?

For 2 reasons: 1) Because prizes can only be offered to verified users and 2) To save your predictions. Your predictions are mapped with the Mobile Number that you provide. Providing your Mobile Number helps you to change your prediction any number of times.
